About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I was born in Rizhao, Shandong, a beautiful costal city. Now I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Advanced Manufacturing and Robotics, Peking University, and I obtained my bachelor's degree in Theoetical and Applied Mechanics from the College of Engineering, Peking University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhisheng Duan. The research fields I'm mainly interested in during my Ph.D. include multi-agent systems, distributed control, optimal control, data-driven and reinforcement learning, as well as safety control. In my spare time, I enjoy computer games, traveling, and savoring delicious food.
